# The Golden Years of Benjamin
## A Photo Book

This is a collection of images of my neighbors' senior dog, Benji, who we dog-sat several times. 

The captions read like a children's story. Our neighbor's children were young and I wanted words that accommodated the pictures to be accessible to them.

I created the album in Google Photos, and ordered a Photo Book. I downloaded a PDF copy and asked Generative AI to create a single static HTML file, no specific prompting.

I made a few edits (I'm a pre-AI developer), uploaded it here and set up a Pages pipeline so it could be easily viewable: https://cdaley78.github.io/benji/.

Benji passed away June 18, 2026.
